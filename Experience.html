Experiences

  Interned at Prime Minister Agriculture Modernization project, sweet orange Superzone, Sindhuli, Nepal| March 2024 - June 2024     
 > Completed a three-month research internship focused on citrus cultivation and pest management.         
 > Conducted survey research on “Farmers' perceptions and control approaches to fruit piercing moth (Eudocima   sps) infestation in sweet orange (Citrus sinensis l.) cultivation: a study in Sindhuli, Nepal”.     
 > Trained in citrus greening detection using the scratch method.
 > Assisted in treatment procedures for controlling fruit flies and citrus red mites.

 pest management protocols.
 > Supported the application of insecticides to control citrus red mites, contributing to effective pest management and fruit   protection.
